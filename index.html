How it works

  1. Command. Hit ⌘Space, type or speak what you want.
  2. Understand. Ractr parses intent plus on-screen context.
  3. Execute. It clicks, types, organizes — or asks before acting.
  4. Verify. You get a tidy summary and an undo stack. Confidence, not chaos.
