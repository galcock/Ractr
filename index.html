How Ractr works

Think of Ractr as a senior game and engine engineer you can talk to. It understands genres, patterns, and production constraints.

1

Describe your game in your own words

Start from a blank canvas or a template. Tell Ractr the kind of game, camera, feel, and progression you want.

“EverQuest-style MMO, stylized, tab-targeting, focus on grouping and dungeons.”

2

Co-build core systems with AI

Ractr generates gameplay code, data structures, and tools. You edit, test, and iterate in the same workspace.

Add new classes, abilities, loot tables, zones, and enemy behaviors in minutes.

3

Plug in networking & deploy

When you’re ready, connect your own backend or use Ractr’s templates as a starting point. Keep full control of your game.

Export clean code into your existing engine, CI, and deployment pipeline.
